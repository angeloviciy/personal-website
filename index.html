I'm a founding engineer at Conway. Conway is an automatic pattern discovery engine that finds what matters in big data.

In a past life, I worked at Infosys' living labs to develop a multi-modal SLM chatbot for industrial clients. I also worked on several computer vision projects at the University of Missouri, where I built high-throughput phenotyping software for the Mittler Lab and modeled objects via photogrammetry with the CREXR Lab.

I had the pleasure of studying CS & Applied Math at Yale University. If you're a bulldog interested in ending up in the bay, I highly encourage you to reach out. I don't bite.
